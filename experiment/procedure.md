##### These procedure steps will be followed on the simulator

1. When you click on the Percolation Rate Test file, a new window will open as shown below.<br>
<img src="images/pr1.png"><br>

2. Choose one of the two types of soil: silty soil or sandy soil.<br>
<img src="images/pr2.png"><br>

3. Click on the funnel to place it on the funnel stand. <br>
<img src="images/pr3.png"><br>

4. Click on the spatula to add the soil sample into the funnel. Then click on the NEXT button.<br>
<img src="images/pr4.png"><br>

5. Click on the graduated cylinder to place it below the funnel.<br>
<img src="images/pr5.png"><br>

6. Take a known quantity of water (e.g., 100 ml) and pour it slowly over the soil in the funnel. Click on the NEXT button to proceed.<br>
<img src="images/pr6.png"><br>

7. Start the stopwatch and note the amount of water collected in the cylinder after a known time. Then click on the CHECK button to calculate the percolation rate.<br>
<img src="images/pr7.png"><br>

8. Click on the CHECK button to calculate the average percolation rate.<br>
<img src="images/pr8.png"><br>

9. The final result will be displayed below.<br>
<img src="images/pr9.png"><br>
